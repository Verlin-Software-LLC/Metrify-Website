Everything counts. You barely have to.

Four ways to log in seconds — and every number is computed with real formulas on your device, not guessed by an AI.

Log by chatting

"Two eggs and toast with butter." Done. Metrify parses what you said, matches it against a 447,000-food USDA catalog, and logs it — no forms, no searching.

Snap a photo

Point your camera at your plate. Metrify identifies what's on it and estimates portions so you can log a whole meal in one shot.

Real math, not vibes

Calories, macros, BMR, and burn estimates are calculated deterministically on your phone. The AI understands you — it doesn't make up your numbers.

Workout Lab

Build routines, track sets and reps, and watch your training history come alive with heatmaps and progress graphs.

Progress that means something

Weight trends with 7-day smoothing, macro breakdowns, and micronutrients — fiber, sodium, iron, and more — at a glance.

Syncs with your watch

Connect Apple Health or Google Health Connect to pull in weight, workouts, steps, and sleep automatically. You approve what gets logged.
